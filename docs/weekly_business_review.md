# Weekly Business Review (WBR)

## Objective

The Weekly Business Review provides executive leadership with a concise view of business performance, operational health, customer activity, and progress against strategic targets.

---

# Executive Summary

Reporting Period

Weekly

Prepared By

Business Intelligence Team

Distribution

* Executive Leadership
* Product Leadership
* Sales Leadership
* Customer Success
* Operations

---

# Key Business Questions

During every review, answer the following questions:

* Are customer activity levels increasing?
* Are KPI targets being achieved?
* Which regions require attention?
* Are operational metrics improving?
* Which initiatives require executive action?

---

# Executive Scorecard

Review

* Active Customers
* Total Activities
* Revenue Target
* Retention Target

Status

* On Track
* At Risk
* Behind Target

---

# Customer Overview

Review

* Customer growth
* Customer activity
* Regional distribution
* Product adoption

Questions

* Which customer segments are growing?
* Which regions require intervention?

---

# Operational Review

Review

* Data refresh status
* Data quality issues
* Missing records
* KPI validation results

Escalate any failed validation before dashboard publication.

---

# KPI Target Review

Compare

* Actual performance
* Monthly target
* Weekly trend

Discuss

* Positive deviations
* Negative deviations
* Required corrective actions

---

# Risks

Monitor

* Declining customer activity
* Missed KPI targets
* Data quality failures
* Reporting delays

Assign an owner and expected resolution date for each identified risk.

---

# Decisions

Record executive decisions made during the meeting.

Example

| Decision                              | Owner            | Due Date    |
| ------------------------------------- | ---------------- | ----------- |
| Increase customer engagement campaign | Customer Success | Next Review |
| Review regional performance           | Operations       | Next Review |

---

# Action Items

Document

* Action description
* Responsible owner
* Target completion date
* Current status

---

# Next Review

Confirm

* Review date
* Outstanding actions
* KPI priorities
* Reporting enhancements

---

# Deliverables

The following outputs should be available after every Weekly Business Review:

* Executive Power BI dashboard
* KPI export workbook
* Regional performance report
* Governance review report
* Meeting notes and action tracker
